Fries

are a popular side dish made from potatoes that are cut into strips and deep-fried until golden brown.

They can be seasoned with salt or various spices and are often served with condiments like ketchup or mayonnaise. Fries can vary in thickness, from thin shoestring fries to thick steak fries, and they are enjoyed worldwide as a tasty snack or accompaniment to meals.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Natural Beef Flavor
  • Dextrose
  • Sodium Acid Pyrophosphate
  • Salt
