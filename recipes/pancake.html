Pancake

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder, cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown. They can be served plain or topped with various ingredients such as syrup, fruit, whipped cream, or butter. Pancakes are enjoyed in many cultures around the world and can vary in thickness and flavor, from classic buttermilk pancakes to more exotic variations like banana or blueberry pancakes.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • Sugar
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • An egg
  • a little butter
  • Milk
