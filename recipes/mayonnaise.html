Mayonnaise

Mayonnaise is a creamy condiment made from emulsifying egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice. It is commonly used as a spread on sandwiches, a base for dressings, and a dip for various foods. Mayonnaise can vary in flavor and texture, with some versions being tangy or flavored with herbs and spices. It is a staple in many cuisines around the world and is often used to enhance the taste of salads, burgers, and other dishes.

Ingredients

  • Soybean oil
  • water
  • whole eggs and egg yolks
  • vinegar
  • salt
  • Sugar
  • lemon juice
  • calcium disodium edta (used to protect quality)
  • natural flavors
